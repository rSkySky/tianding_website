Our Services

  • Require goods to be shipped from China to Malta?

    We have partnered with China Ocean Shipping Company (COSCO) and Shanghai Maxiong Industrial Co., Ltd. to help transport your goods to Malta, quickly, safely and simply.

    Our mission at Tianding International Co.,Ltd is to provide our clients, whether that be store-owners, venue organizers, renovators or general populace, with efficient shipping at a competitive price. We provide weekly shipments, sea delivery only taking 25~35 days to arrive.

    Clients can buy goods from stores and companies exclusively found in China, then send us an order, and we take care of the shipping to Malta.

  • Our China to Malta shipping details and rates

    We provide weekly shipments, sea delivery only taking 25~35 days to arrive, and goods covered by marine insurance with loss or damage claims handled according to international insurance terms.

    - Sea shipping reduced to 53 EUR (400 RMB) per standard box (40 x 50 x 60 cm), weight limit 30 kg per box.

    - Non-standard boxes are calculated at 440 EUR (3350 RMB) per cubic meter, weight limit 250 kg per cubic meter.

    - Express delivery and packing service (customer packages are not unpacked, service fee 2 EUR (15 RMB) per box).

    - Please contact customer service to obtain a warehouse entry number, 7 EUR (50 RMB) per time, and recieve a discounted door-to-door delivery service.

    If extra details are required, send us an e-mail
    CONTACT US







  • Goods storage and delivery

    After arriving in Malta, the warehouse will store them for free for one week. After one week, each standard box will be charged 1 euro/week, so please collect your goods as soon as possible.

    Free delivery for large containers, 7 euros per box for small container shipping. Due to the high delivery cost of small containers, free delivery cannot be provided; if large containers are shipped, free door-to-door delivery is provided.

    The above quotation is only for standard containers within 30kg, non-standard containers or overweight containers are quoted according to actual conditions, contact us for more details or for any queries.
